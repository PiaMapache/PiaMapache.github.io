Who is this person?

Pia is a 27 years old Chilean, half self-taught, half academically-taught Visual Artist focused on diverse digital media techniques, including illustrations and concept art.
Her art is mainly focused on representing the human body, such as face-centered portraits, semi-realistic anime, constant anatomy studies and a bit of erotica in some NSFW works.

She takes commissionns for illustrations, you can reach her via email, or following one of the links at the top of the page!
